Imagine Nosara could produce 1,000 mangos every year.

867 are lost before they reach the basket; stolen, hidden, left to rot, or never picked.

The Municipality takes what remains, and gives back just 1 mango to Nosara.

Out of every 1,000 mangos, only 1 stays in the district.

That is not a metaphor; it is fiscal injustice.
